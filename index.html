Our Story

Guests describe it as a place of genuine peace — pocket-friendly, spacious, and infused with a refreshing, positive aura that stays with you long after you leave.

What Awaits You

Curated Experiences

Every moment at The Viraaj is designed to slow you down, lift your spirit, and reconnect you with nature and the people you love.

🌄

Hill Sunrise Views

Wake to the magical sight of dawn breaking over the ancient Aravali ridgeline. Golden light spills across the valley in a display nature offers only to those who slow down enough to notice.

🌿

Farm Walks & Nature Trails

Stroll through verdant farmland, breathe in the pure country air, and discover local flora along gentle trails that wind through the Mangar woodland — a world apart from city dust.

🍃

Outdoor Dining & Bonfires

Gather around an open fire under a canopy of stars. Share meals prepared with fresh local produce, as laughter echoes across the hills and time stretches generously around you.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Family & Group Retreats

Spacious grounds perfect for families and friend groups to unwind together. Our elegant, attentive staff ensure every guest feels genuinely welcome and effortlessly cared for.

🧘

Yoga & Wellness

Let the hillside breeze and birdsong accompany your morning practice. The Viraaj's open spaces and positive energy create a natural backdrop for mindfulness and renewal.

📸

Photography & Sunsets

The Aravali Hills offer endlessly photogenic moments — dramatic skies, rolling green terrain, rustic village textures, and sunsets that paint the horizon in hues of amber and rose.
